About Me

With over 20 years' experience in fire safety, I provide independent, professional fire risk assessments to landlords, businesses and property managers across [COVERAGE_AREA]. I hold qualifications recognised by the Fire Protection Association (FPA) and stay current with all relevant fire safety legislation.

Unlike larger consultancies, every assessment I undertake is carried out by me personally, from initial conversation through to final report. This means you deal directly with the person who knows your property and can answer your questions without being passed between departments.

My approach is practical rather than bureaucratic: I focus on proportionate, sensible recommendations that genuinely improve safety and help you meet your legal obligations, without unnecessary cost or complexity.

Services

Commercial Premises

Fire risk assessments for offices, workshops and commercial units, tailored to occupancy and use. Clear, actionable recommendations for compliance and safety.

Residential Blocks

Communal area assessments for blocks of flats, covering means of escape, compartmentation and management arrangements in line with current guidance.

HMOs

Specialist assessments for Houses in Multiple Occupation, addressing the particular fire safety challenges of shared living accommodation.

Schools

Practical assessments for educational settings, balancing statutory requirements with the day-to-day realities of a busy school environment.

Retail

Assessments for shops and retail units of all sizes, considering customer footfall, stock storage and staff procedures.

Warehouses

Thorough assessments for storage and distribution premises, including consideration of stored materials and fire load.
